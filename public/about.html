Windsor Census Maps

A website showing various data elements from the 2016 Canadian Census on a map of Windsor, Ontario


About the author

The website was built by John Haldeman, who lives in Windsor, as a way to learn more about React, visualizing data on maps, and Windsor. You can connect to John on LinkedIn and Github.
